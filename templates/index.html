About this project

This application provides a web-based front-end for managing the Transport Office database. It supports full CRUD operations across 9 tables — citizen records, vehicles, driving licenses, vehicle registrations, violations, taxes, payments, liabilities, and payment linkages. All queries use raw SQL with parameterized placeholders. Foreign key constraints are enforced and handled gracefully with user-friendly error messages.
